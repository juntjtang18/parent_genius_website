Frequently Asked Questions

About GeniusParentingAI

GeniusParentingAI was founded by an educator and a software professional who combine expertise in child development, parenting education, technology, and lived parenting experience.

It was built from a simple belief:

Parenting deserves thoughtful, judgment-free support — especially in multicultural environments like Canada.

This platform was created not from theory alone, but from real parenting challenges and real classroom experience.

Our mission is to support parents with:

  • Calm, reflective guidance
  • Culturally aware parenting insights
  • Ethical, privacy-first technology
  • A supportive, inclusive community

We believe confident parenting grows from understanding — not pressure.

Who Is It For?

GeniusParentingAI is designed for:

  • Parents and caregivers

  • Educators and practitioners

You do not need to be a “perfect parent” to belong here.

AI in GeniusParentingAI is designed to:

  • Help parents reflect on situations
  • Offer different perspectives
  • Support thoughtful decision-making

It does NOT:

  • Diagnose children
  • Replace professional advice
  • Make decisions for families

AI is a thinking partner — not a replacement for human care.
